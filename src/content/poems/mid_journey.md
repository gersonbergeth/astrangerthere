title: "Mid Journey"
author: "Gerson Bergeth"
date: "30/09/2023"
location: "Oslo"
published: true
---
First thing I saw was the eyes
The rest was covered 
It was the time of plague 
Everyone was covered 

They drew me in, the eyes
Already I wanted more 
But both were cautions 
Time had me wait for more 

On the shoreline a smile 
A match to the gaze 
Half-moon boats floating
I moved closing the space

We spoke little, not much 
But shared mountains 
Our possessions were few
Yet the rain filled fountains 

Not much divided us 
Only floors, doors, stairs 
In the chaos that is there 
We had time in spares

Had we gone on a bit longer 
Just a week a month an hour 
Had she met the next stage of me
Had I been to the next level of she 
But it was not meant to be 

It never is mid-journey
