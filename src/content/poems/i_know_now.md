---
title: "I Know Now"
author: "Gerson Bergeth"
date: "09/09/2020"
location: "Cox's Bazar"
published: true
---
I know now where you came from 
Not just your country, city and flag
I know now where you want to be 
Not only a home, a kindred and family 

I know now whom you’d like to meet 
More than a man or a person with limbs
I know now what you need in the morning 
Beyond the caresses and chests heaving   

I know now why you argue so
Defending but mainly attacking 
I know now who you’d rather won           
The moon cannot hide from your sun 

I know now that pain is your friend 
Brave princess that holds up the crown 
I know now that wrong can be light 
Standing ready when others take flight 

I know now the music of sounds
My timpanus still rattles from April 
I know now that to dance we must fall 
The tribe forms a line at the beat call 

I know now the attempt was made 
With a smile, a laugh and a long drink 
I know now that desperation is here 
To live fully and to stare without fear

I know now that your memory will stay 
The still frames, the moving frames 
I know now a page is written indelibly  
Paths traverse but you I take with me 

PS: You always knew what I know now.
