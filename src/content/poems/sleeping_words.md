---
title: "Sleeping Words"
author: "Gerson Bergeth"
date: "01/10/2017"
location: "Bangkok"
published: true
---
Where did all my writings go? 
Lost in the rush of change 
Misplaced in the bid for a prize 
Many words now wandering aimless

As shepherd I must gather them 
Bring then home into my warm brain 
Back to fervent thoughts and images
In group they are happy, we are happy 

I remember their place on a page 
Orphans I took, reared and disciplined 
My young, my lovely fellows of the print 
Born of pen and sword and byte 

It has been many moons since I birthed 
Crying into earth they came naked
With siblings they sang in unison
To my tune they hummed a paragraph 

I am sick for writing, composing, birthing 
My fingers are ready and eyes focused 
A need I have to create an edifice 
To build a dimension on paper

The muses are standing by ready 
Purple and yellow fumes fill my head 
I see a character emerging suddenly
Grab it, own it and nail it down with words 

This is a good place for inspiration 
My skin is tingling and feet floating 
Take the words and manage the concepts 
Or they will be lost again and I recede 

And sleep
