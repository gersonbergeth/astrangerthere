---
title: "Anthem-like"
author: "Gerson Bergeth"
date: "01/10/2001"
location: "Jakarta"
published: true
---
You and I, we, us 

Shall come together 
With the father of war 
The mother of tears 
The daughter of solitude 
And the son of aggression 

Us and the four 
Will find a solution 
To the history of man 
The recurring tragic act
That made war an inevitable fact 

We will sit at a round table
All as equals, as one
Without heads or leaders 
On the level, in honesty 
Without concern for self-security 

Progress we will make 
No vision of currency or trade 
Advance we will, unstoppable 
Temporary negations not to be spoken 
With a clear memory of life our, token 

And the together 
With the father of peace
The mother of joy 
The daughter of unity 
And the son of tolerance 

We shall burn the last gun 
Melt the last sword 
Sink the last tank 
Bury the last bullet 
Forget the last cannon 

You and I, we, us
